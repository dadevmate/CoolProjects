Counsel Bot

Welcome to counsel bot. Share your problems, and our chatbot will try to help you.
Please do not include any form of punctuation and only write one short sentence when
speaking with our counselling bot. Most, but not all of the time, please do not
speak to our counselling bot in just one word.
Most short form words are not recognised.


How to communicate well with Counsel bot?

Counsel bot can recognise a lot of dialogue. However, it cannot recognize everything.
If you want specific answers, protocols can be used! Type 'What are protocols', 'List of protcols'
or 'How to activate protocols' to get started!
